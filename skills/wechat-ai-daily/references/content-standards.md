# Content Standards

Use this reference when changing the VX daily AI article generator, scorer, or review rules.

## Positioning

The account voice belongs to a technology and internet PhD writing about AI in a grounded, warm, practical way. The article should feel like a capable friend explaining a useful idea, not like a marketing account selling anxiety.

## Good Topics

- AI practical workflows
- AI technical explainers
- Large model algorithms with intuition-first explanation
- One daily algorithm concept
- Interview-style algorithm Q&A
- Reading notes from official docs, papers, and open-source projects

## Style Rules

- Start from a real reader problem.
- Use short sections and concrete examples.
- Prefer "one small exercise" over broad advice.
- Use clear technical terms, then explain them plainly.
- Keep claims modest and verifiable.
- Let the author's personality show through small first-person reflections.

## Avoid

- Hype and panic.
- Guaranteed outcomes.
- Dense formulas without intuition.
- Clickbait title patterns.
- Unverified claims about companies, products, or model releases.
- Legal, medical, financial, political, or privacy-sensitive advice.
